How to do Discord webhooks | samscodz


In this case, we will make the webhook to literally send anything.


How to do it?

First thing, go to your Discord server and go to server settings. And then, in the Apps section, go to Integrations. Click Create Webhook. After that, a brand new webhook will be created. Name it whatever you want, send to whatever channel you want, and after customization click "Copy Webhook URL". After that, go to Discohook, paste your webhook URL (that you just copied) there, customise the things you want to send and just click "Send" besides the Webhook URL input. Your webhook will be sent. That's all!
